## ğŸ’¡ Key Design Principles

1. **Separation of Concerns**: Strategic vs Operational
2. **Visual Distinction**: Gold/Amber for executive, Green for operational
3. **Information Density**: High-level summary for minister, detailed for admin
4. **Access Control**: Minister cannot access operational details
5. **Focus**: Minister = "What should we achieve?", Admin = "How do we do it?"
