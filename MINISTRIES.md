# Royal Government of Cambodia - Official Ministries
## រាជរដ្ឋាភិបាលកម្ពុជា - ក្រសួងផ្លូវការ

This document lists the official ministries of the Royal Government of Cambodia integrated into the Ministry Document Tracking System.

## Overview

Based on official sources from the Royal Government of Cambodia, there are **28 government ministries and secretariats** that serve the Kingdom of Cambodia. This document tracking system includes the major ministries.

## Integrated Ministries (12 Main Ministries)

### 1. Ministry of Economy and Finance
**Khmer:** ក្រសួងសេដ្ឋកិច្ច និងហិរញ្ញវត្ថុ
**English:** Ministry of Economy and Finance
**Responsibilities:** National budget, financial policies, economic development, taxation

### 2. Ministry of Health
**Khmer:** ក្រសួងសុខាភិបាល
**English:** Ministry of Health
**Responsibilities:** Healthcare services, public health policies, medical regulations

### 3. Ministry of Education, Youth and Sport
**Khmer:** ក្រសួងអប់រំ យុវជន និងកីឡា
**English:** Ministry of Education, Youth and Sport
**Responsibilities:** Education curriculum, schools, universities, youth development, sports programs

### 4. Ministry of Interior
**Khmer:** ក្រសួងមហាផ្ទៃ
**English:** Ministry of Interior
**Responsibilities:** Internal security, local administration, civil affairs, immigration

### 5. Ministry of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation
**Khmer:** ក្រសួងការបរទេស និងសហប្រតិបត្តិការអន្តរជាតិ
**English:** Ministry of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation
**Responsibilities:** Diplomatic relations, international cooperation, consular services

### 6. Ministry of Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries
**Khmer:** ក្រសួងកសិកម្ម រុក្ខាប្រមាញ់ និងនេសាទ
**English:** Ministry of Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries
**Responsibilities:** Agricultural development, forestry management, fisheries regulation

### 7. Ministry of Labour and Vocational Training
**Khmer:** ក្រសួងការងារ និងបណ្តុះបណ្តាលវិជ្ជាជីវៈ
**English:** Ministry of Labour and Vocational Training
**Responsibilities:** Employment, worker rights, vocational training, labor regulations

### 8. Ministry of Commerce
**Khmer:** ក្រសួងពាណិជ្ជកម្ម
**English:** Ministry of Commerce
**Responsibilities:** Trade policies, business licensing, consumer protection, commerce development

### 9. Ministry of Justice
**Khmer:** ក្រសួងយុត្តិធម៌
**English:** Ministry of Justice
**Responsibilities:** Legal system, courts, law enforcement, judicial affairs

### 10. Ministry of Information
**Khmer:** ក្រសួងព័ត៌មាន
**English:** Ministry of Information
**Responsibilities:** Media regulation, public information, broadcasting, press affairs

### 11. Ministry of Planning
**Khmer:** ក្រសួងផែនការ
**English:** Ministry of Planning
**Responsibilities:** National development planning, strategic planning, statistics

### 12. Ministry of Public Works and Transport
**Khmer:** ក្រសួងសាធារណការ និងដឹកជញ្ជូន
**English:** Ministry of Public Works and Transport
**Responsibilities:** Infrastructure, roads, transportation, construction

## Government Structure

### Royal Government of Cambodia
**Khmer:** រាជរដ្ឋាភិបាលកម្ពុជា
**Led by:** Prime Minister (នាយករដ្ឋមន្ត្រី)

### Council of Ministers
**Khmer:** គណៈរដ្ឋមន្ត្រី
**English:** Council of Ministers

The Council of Ministers is the executive body of the Kingdom of Cambodia, headed by the Prime Minister and includes:
- Deputy Prime Ministers
- Senior Ministers
- Ministers
- Secretaries of State

## User Roles in System

### 1. Administrator (អភិបាល)
- Full system access
- Can manage all ministries
- System configuration

### 2. Ministry Secretary (លេខាធិការក្រសួង)
- Ministry-level management
- Department oversight
- Document approval authority

### 3. Officer (មន្ត្រី)
- Upload and download documents
- Department-level access
- Standard operations

### 4. Viewer (អ្នកមើល)
- Read-only access
- Download with permission
- View-only capabilities
